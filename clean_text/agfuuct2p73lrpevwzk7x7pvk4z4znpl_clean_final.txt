UZBEKISTAN; AND NOTICE OF ITS PRELIMINARY DETERMINATION OF SALES AT NOT LESS THAN FAIR VALUE REGARDING URANIUM FROM ARMENIA, AZERBAIJAN, BYELARUS, GEORGIA, MOLDOVA, AND TURKMENISTAN. THE PRODUCTS COVERED BY THESE INVESTIGATIONS INCLUDE NATURAL URANIUM IN THE FORM OF URANIUM ORES AND CONCENTRATES; NATURAL URANIUM METAL AND NATURAL URANIUM COMPOUNDS; ALLOYS, DISPERSIONS (INCLUDING CERMETS), CERAMIC PRODUCTS AND MIXTURES CONTAINING NATURAL URANIUM OR NATURAL URANIUM COMPOUNDS; URANIUM ENRICHED IN U235 AND ITS COMPOUNDS; ALLOYS, DISPERSIONS (INCLUDING CERMETS), CERAMIC PRODUCTS, AND MIXTURES CONTAINING URANIUM ENRICHED IN U235 OR COMPOUNDS OF URANIUM ENRICHED IN U235. HIGHLY ENRICHED URANIUM, WHICH IS URANIUM ENRICHED TO A U235 CONCENTRATION LEVEL AT OR ABOVE 20 PERCENT, IS EXCLUDED FROM THE SCOPE OF THESE INVESTIGATIONS. THE URANIUM SUBJECT TO THESE INVESTIGATIONS IS PROVIDED FOR UNDER SUBHEADINGS 2612.10.00.00, 2844.10.10.00, 10.20.10, 2844.10.20.25, 2844.10.20.50, 2844.10.20.55, 10.50.00, 2844.20.00.10, 2844.20.00.20, 2844.20.00.30, AND 20.00.50, OF THE HARMONIZED TARIFF SCHEDULE (HTS). ALTHOUGH THE HTS SUBHEADINGS ARE PROVIDED FOR CONVENIENCE AND CUSTOMS PURPOSES, THE WRITTEN DESCRIPTION OF THE SCOPE OF THESE PROCEEDINGS IS DISPOSITIVE. THE U.S. CUSTOMS SERVICE SHALL SUSPEND LIQUIDATION OF ALL ENTRIES OF URANIUM FROM KAZAKHSTAN, KYRGYZSTAN, RUSSIA, TAJIKISTAN, UKRAINE AND UZBEKISTAN THAT ARE ENTERED, OR WITHDRAWN FROM WAREHOUSE, FOR CONSUMPTION ON OR AFTER MARCH 5, 1992, WHICH IS 90 DAYS PRIOR TO THE DATE OF PUBLICATION OF THIS NOTICE IN THE FEDERAL REGISTER DUE TO THE DETERMINATION THAT CRITICAL CIRCUMSTANCES EXIST. THE U.S. CUSTOMS SERVICE SHALL REQUIRE A CASH DEPOSIT OR THE POSTING OF A BOND EQUAL TO 115.82 PERCENT. DUE TO THE DEPARTMENT'S PRELIMINARY NEGATIVE DETERMINATIONS WITH RESPECT TO ARMENIA, AZERBAIJAN, BYELARUS, GEORGIA, MOLDOVA, AND TURKMENISTAN, CUSTOMS SHALL NOT SUSPEND LIQUIDATION OF ENTRIES OF URANIUM FROM THESE COUNTRIES.
