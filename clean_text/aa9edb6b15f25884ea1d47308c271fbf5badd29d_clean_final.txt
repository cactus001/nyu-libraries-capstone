The Humanitarian Data Exchange (HDX) is an open platform for sharing data across platforms and organizations. The goal of HDX is to make humanitarian data easy to find and use for analysis. Launched in July 2014, HDX has been accessed by millions of users in over 250 countries and territories and brings together more than 20,500 datasets from over 290 partner organizations. The Humanitarian Exchange Language A simple standard for messy data The Humanitarian Exchange Language (HXL) is a data standard.
