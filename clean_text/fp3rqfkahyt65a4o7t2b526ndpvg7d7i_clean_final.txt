635 Effect on conflicting laws. All sections of the Kentucky Revised Statutes in conflict with KRS 15.605 to 15.630 are repealed to the extent that they are inconsistent with KRS 15.605 to 15.630. Effective: July 1, 1978 History: Created 1976 Ky. Acts ch. 46, sec. 7, effective July 1, 1978.
