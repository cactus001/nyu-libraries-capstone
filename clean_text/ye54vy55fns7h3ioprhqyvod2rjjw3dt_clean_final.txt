Student Handout Name:____________ _____________________ Human blood observation Directions: Obtain a slide of prepared human blood. Start on low power (10x or 20x) and work your way to high power (40x), focusing under each objective. Under high power, draw a portion of what you see. Use a pencil and pay attention to detail. Drawing: Questions: How many types of cells do you see? What is the difference between them? Why do you think there are different types of cells? What is the composition of the cells that you see? In other words, estimate the percent of each type.
