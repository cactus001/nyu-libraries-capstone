proposed rate increase. Hazard Perry County Community Ministries objects to this proposed rate increase because little to nothing is known about its justification. Kentucky Power has not explained how this will improve its service, or why they cannot make ends meet with the current rates in place. Because Kentucky Power basically has a monopoly on their industry in this region, it is imperative that they be required to make its customers aware of the reasoning behind this rate increase of astronomical proportions. Without proper checks and balances such as requiring Kentucky Power to provide the basis for the requested increase and allowing affected entities to address them, the potential for abuse is great. Hazard Perry County Community Ministries serves mostly low income individuals Most of the people who live in its rental housing are fbrthering their education and/or working low wage jobs. These individuals can barely afford to pay their electricity hills now. With a proposed rate increase of 33.430/0, they will be face with an untenable choice-whether they can keep the lights on or keep their children fed. In addition, Hazard Perry County Community Ministries has a unique perspective on the utility market in Perry County. As part of the services provided, families come to Hazard Perry County Community Ministries with electric bills in arrears or shutoff notices. Hazard Perry County Community Ministries then can step in with its homelessness prevention assistance. In 2009, Hazard Perry County Community Ministries served approximately 100 individuals with $5,000 toward Kentucky Power bill assistance. With the proposed rate increase, it is estimated that the number of people requesting assistance will double. However, Hazard Perry County Community Minisitriest will not have the funds to intervene on behalf of all of those seeking assistance. Both the families who are of 6
